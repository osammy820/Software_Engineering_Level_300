2. Compare and contrast the Waterfall and Agile software process models. 
What are the key differences between the two models and 
what are the benefits and drawbacks of each?
   ANS: Both models are used for software development.
    In Waterfall software process models,It is linear,sequential approach to software development where each phase of the developmental process is completed before moving onto the next phase Whiles
     In Agile software models, It is an incremental,iterative approach to software development that involves working in short iterations.
1. In Waterfall, it is rigid process WHILES In Agile, it is flexible process.
    2. In Waterfall, it is linear,sequential approach WHILES In Agile, it is incermental,iterative approach.
       Benefits of WaterFall software process model
    1. it provides a clear and defined structure for the software development process.
    2. it is easier to plan and manage.
        Drawbacks of WaterFall software process model
    1.  it has limited flexibility.
    2.  it is less suitable for projects where customer involvement and feedback are crucial.
        Benefits of Agile software process model
    1.  It is flexible.
    2.  More Rapid delivery and deployment of useful software to the customer is possible.
        Drawbacks Of Agile software process model
    1.  The process is not visible.
    2.   System structure tends to degrade as new increments are added.


3. What is Rational Unified Process (RUP), and 
how does it differ from other software development process models? 
What are the key phases and activities involved in RUP, and 
what are the benefits and drawbacks of using this approach in software development projects?
 ANS: Rational Unified Process is an iterative and incremental software development process framework which emphasizes the important of adapting the development process to the specific needs of a project.And what differs from RUP is that it focus on adapting the development process to the specific needs of a project.
 The key phases include;
   *Inception
   *Elaboration
   *Construction
   *Transition
  Benefits
  1. Improved quality.
  2. It ensures flexibility.
   Drawbacks
  1.  It is highly structured and prescriptive process that can be inflexible in some situation.
  2.  It is complex and highly structured process framework that requires significant time and effort to implement.
